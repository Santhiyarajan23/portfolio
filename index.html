About Me

My journey from code to creative problem-solving.

Bridging User Needs & Technical Execution

I'm a UX designer with a Bachelor's in Computer Applications (BCA), which gives me a unique advantage: I don't just create user-friendly designs; I understand how to build them. This allows me to effectively collaborate with developers and ensure design feasibility.

I am passionate about the entire design process—from understanding the 'why' behind user behavior through research, to iterating on wireframes and prototypes, and validating solutions through testing. I believe in building products that are not just functional, but truly accessible and delightful to use.

Problem-First Mindset

I focus on defining the right problem before jumping to solutions, ensuring design efforts have maximum impact.

Collaborative by Nature
